About TechNova Electronics

Our Story

Started by two engineers who hated bad spec sheets

TechNova Electronics began in 2016 in a two-room office above a repair shop, founded by engineers frustrated with electronics retailers who buried the specs that actually mattered under marketing language. We started by selling refurbished laptops with honest, plain-English condition reports.

A decade later, that same principle drives every listing on this site: real specs, real stock counts, and real photos of what you're actually buying. We've since expanded into smartphones, wearables, audio, and gaming gear — but the spec sheet still comes first, every time.

Today we work directly with manufacturers and authorized distributors across three continents, which is how we're able to keep prices honest without cutting corners on warranty support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Orders placed before 3pm ship the same day. Standard delivery takes 2–5 business days depending on your location.

You can return any unopened or defective product within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Do you offer warranty on refurbished items? +

Yes — every product, new or refurbished, ships with at least a 1-year limited warranty.

Orders can be changed or cancelled within 1 hour of placement by contacting our support team.
